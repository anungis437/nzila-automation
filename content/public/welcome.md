# Welcome to Nzila

Nzila is a digital-venture studio building a portfolio of interconnected platforms across multiple verticals — from legal-tech and fintech to cybersecurity education and cultural media.

## Our Approach

We operate as a **multi-vertical platform company**, sharing a common technology backbone across all products:

- **Shared infrastructure** — Authentication, billing, analytics, and deployment pipelines are built once and reused.
- **Vertical specialization** — Each product targets a specific market with deep domain expertise.
- **Data-driven iteration** — Every platform feeds insights back into a central analytics engine.
