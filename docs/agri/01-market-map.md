# Agri Stack — Market Map

## Target Markets

### Primary: Sub-Saharan Africa

- **Coffee cooperatives** (DRC, Rwanda, Uganda, Ethiopia, Kenya)
- **Cocoa aggregators** (Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, Cameroon)
- **Cashew exporters** (Mozambique, Tanzania, Guinea-Bissau)
- **Cotton cooperatives** (Mali, Burkina Faso, Benin)
- **Sesame and soy traders** (Nigeria, Ethiopia, Sudan)

### Secondary: Latin America & Southeast Asia

- **Coffee cooperatives** (Colombia, Guatemala, Honduras)
- **Palm oil aggregators** (Indonesia, Malaysia)
- **Spice cooperatives** (India, Vietnam)

## Buyer Personas

| Persona | Need | Pondu Value | Cora Value |
|---------|------|-------------|------------|
| **Cooperative Manager** | Track harvests, manage producer payments | End-to-end lot tracking, automated payment distribution | Yield benchmarking, producer performance |
| **Export Compliance Officer** | Certification + traceability for buyers | Sealed evidence packs, audit trail | Impact reports, traceability chain visualization |
| **Commodity Trader** | Inventory visibility, shipment tracking | Warehouse management, shipment milestones | Price signal monitoring, risk dashboards |
| **Agri-Finance Lender** | Collateral verification, payout visibility | Certified lot records, payment plans | Portfolio risk scoring, yield forecasts |
| **Development Agency (DFI)** | Impact measurement, smallholder traceability | Producer records, certification artifacts | Cooperative performance, impact analytics |

## Competitive Landscape

| Category | Competitors | NzilaOS Advantage |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Farm management | Farmforce, Dimitra, AgroStar | Org-isolated multi-tenant + evidence packs |

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Target | Pondu | Cora | Bundle |
|------|--------|-------|------|--------|
| **Starter** | Small cooperatives (<500 producers) | ✅ | ✅ Basic | ✅ |
| **Professional** | Mid-size exporters | ✅ | ✅ Full | ✅ |
| **Enterprise** | Large aggregators, trading houses | ✅ + API | ✅ + Custom models | ✅ + SLA |

## Revenue Streams

1. **SaaS subscriptions** — per-org monthly/annual
2. **Transaction fees** — per-shipment or per-certification
3. **Data services** — anonymized market intelligence (opt-in)
4. **Professional services** — integration, migration, training
5. **Sovereign deployments** — license + support
